Report:《Pedigree:How Elite Students Get Elite Jobs》《出身》阅读笔记

Report: 《Pedigree: How Elite Students Get Elite Jobs》

《出身：不平等的选拔与精英的自我复制》阅读笔记

Written by Lauren A. Rivera.

美国的一名出身贫穷的博士生，走进美国EPS (Elite Professional Service)公司：顶级投资银行、管理咨询公司、律师事务所开展案例研究，从面试者的背景、学校、面试结果着手分析“精英到底是如何产生的”。

My Report

摘抄

面试看重的业余活动

“沿着布尔迪厄“非必需性”的逻辑，这些工作需要父母和子女将大量的时间、精力和金钱投入到并不立即有用或有成效的活动中去，在业余活动的选择上尤为明显。他们既需要掌握内部信息，能够确认这些符号的价值，也要有时间和金钱正确地发展这些符号。”

——成功说明，在面试中，打动人的业余活动成效与家庭的经济状况和社会地位息息相关。

受阶级品味主导的文化符号

“他们所喜欢的符号—知名学府的文凭、高地位的课外活动、光鲜的互动风格，以及关于热情、自立、自我实现的个人叙事—都不是艺术的或高雅的，但的确与阶层有关。”

经济地位的延续性机制

“经济地位完全靠个人努力挣得，在这一流行叙事的背后，有一个发达的机制，将一代人的经济特权传递给下一代。这一机制首先如前人研究已经证明的，将富裕的孩子输送到有保障的大学里，然后如我的研究所揭示的，引领他们进入蓝筹公司和最高收入阶层。”

扎根于社会建构的优点阐释

“优点不是一个人身上固定的、能随时在不同场合表现出来的内在属性，而是一种社会建构，植根于社会在特定的时间和地点，对于什么构成了价值的文化理念。”

结论

“理论上，这些公司的招聘实践是与阶层无关的，顶级公司的招聘者们只是想要聘用“最优秀、最聪明的人”；

但实际上，正如本书所揭示的，这些公司评估应聘者价值的方式以及做出聘用决定的方式极大地倾斜了顶级工作竞争的天平，更偏向来自社会经济特权家庭的学生。

公司定义才能的方式将表现优异但较少特权背景的学生排除在外，其筛选应聘者和做出最终决定所依据的指标与父母的社会经济地位密切相关。

正是由于他们招聘采用的方式，这些公司系统地排除了那些聪明、有动力、社交娴熟但没有特权背景的学生，使他们无缘美国收入最高的入门级工作，这些岗位是进入国家经济精英阶层的入口。

如此一来，更加剧了美国社会分层体系的日益固化，从一个人的社会出身便可预言他最终的社会位置，向上流动也不如在其他西方工业国家那样常见。”

——阶级固化的逻辑链形成闭环。

另：一些个人想法

“出身”也许是在整本书所呈现的社会现象下最被看重的个人特质，无论是学历资质，活动经历，都建立在“出身”于怎样的家庭，怎样的经济背景和社会背景之下。

可出身也恰恰是我们无法自行选择，无法通过努力来实现的。所以，有人会认为，即便怎样努力，最终都只是在现处的阶级中争得更高一点的地位，而无法实现真正的阶级跨越。

确实是这样，阶级跨越的实现很难，因为阶级不同而无法获得真正有用的文化符号而在教育，就业之中被能够实现阶级转换的台阶绊住脚。

但这不意味着我们没有可以去努力的方向。在面试过程中，仍有机会匹配到非经济特权出身的面试官；在个人经历的欠缺之中，也不意味着我们做到尽自己所能展示对于职位的热情就会遭到拒绝。在各个环节，都存在着机遇和可能。

基本归因错误（fundamental attribution error）一词阐述人们在解释发生在自己身上的好事时普遍存在的心理偏好，认为这些事情源于自身内在的稳定品质（如勤奋）而不是外在因素（如运气）。相反，解释失败时，认为是由个人的内在缺陷（如智力不足）导致的。而正好相反，个人的努力勤奋也许不一定真正换来好的结果，就像大学努力学习拿到GPA4.0的学生可能会由于疏忽活动而被拒绝，在面试的抉择之中，人与人之间的交流会带来机遇，运气成分也占有很大一部分成功的比例。

在无法改变社会大环境的情况下，能够做的是尽力适应，以自己所能企及的最大程度去达到精英标准，在一定运气的加持下，在正确的方向上努力，加强自己对于所需特质的培养，成功也并非难事。

“So we beat on, boats against the current, borne back ceaselessly into the past.”

“我们继续前行，如舟逆流而上，不断被无尽的过去牵引回去。”

The three years of high school may swiftly pass, with the first third already drawn to a close. As I spend my fulfilling summer vacation, I write this memoir—a summary, a reflection, and a contemplation laid out for a fresh beginning.

高中三年的时光，前三分之一已然落下帷幕。当我正在度过我充实的暑假，我写下这篇回忆录，亦是总结，亦是回望，亦是为焕然新生而布下的反思。

As I attempt to reminisce, I recall the sweat, laughter, joy, and bitterness I harvested this year—all accompanying experiences that have contributed to my growth. I believe it’s necessary to document each of them as milestones for future reflections on life. I will recount each experience of this year.

当我尝试去回忆，我曾在这一年收获的汗水，欢笑，喜悦，苦涩，它们都伴随着使我成长的阅历。我想有必要将它们一一记录，作为日后人生回望的里程碑。我将一一道来，这一年的经历。

The Military Training Life - Experiences as a Flag Squad Member & Flag Bearer军训生活之——国旗班&护旗手经历

“Whether under scorching sun or heavy rain, keep practicing persistently like this.”

“烈日骄阳也好，大雨也好，就这样坚持练呗。”

​ ——During training…My dear teammates

​ ——来自训练时…我亲爱的队员们

The 6-day, 5-night journey, unforgettable moments spent with new classmates, standing in military formation under the scorching sun. Perhaps military training is indeed a great opportunity; despite the hardships and tears, it strengthens camaraderie. I feel fortunate to have met such companions during this time.

6天5晚的旅程，无法忘记和新认识的同学们在一起相处，顶着大太阳站军姿练队列的那些日子。或许军训真是一个好时机，虽然有苦有泪，但确实是一个增强团魂的好时机，我也幸运在这样的时间里结识了伙伴。

On the second day of division, I was fortunate to become a member of the military training flag squad. The task was honorable, the training rigorous. We represented the square, entrusted with the mission to uphold the dignity of the national flag. On the final day of the freshman orientation ceremony, we raised and guarded the flag, showcasing the formation to the entire grade. I contributed my part as a flag bearer.

在第二天的分队之中，我便有幸成为了军训国旗班的一名成员。任务是光荣的，训练是刻苦的。我们所代表的方阵，肩负着守卫国旗尊严的使命，在最后一天的新生汇报仪式上升旗护旗，代表全年级展示方阵队列。而我更是在其中尽到我自己的一份力：我成为一名护旗手。

During the final performance when raising the national flag, I had the privilege of appearing in the school’s recorded video as the flag bearer (far left)

And these are the results of diligent practice repeated over six days of training.

而这些，是在6天的训练中，反反复复刻苦练习的成果。

Although the military training took place at the end of August, we couldn’t escape the impact of the scorching summer days. During this period, we not only endured intense heat and sun exposure every day but also faced sudden thunderstorms. Despite these challenges, our training never slackened. On the contrary, our enthusiasm grew stronger. Even on stormy nights, our spirits soared, and we persevered in training, rain or shine.

军训的时间虽在8月底，但依然不免于炎炎夏日带来的冲击。在这期间，不仅仅是每天的酷暑骄阳暴晒，还遇上雷阵雨的洗劫。但尽管如此，我们的训练也未曾松懈过，更甚于此，同伴们的热情更为高涨，在暴雨肆虐的夜晚，我们的心却更加澎湃，即便是冒雨也坚持训练。

We primarily trained in marching, similar to a honor guard, practicing synchronized steps. Precision was the greatest test of our unity, and achieving this was our constant goal through continuous practice.

我们主要训练的是如同仪仗队一般的正步，齐步练习，整齐度是对我们团结的最大考验。这也是我们不断练习所想要达到的目标。

Fire Station Career Experience消防站职业体验

It is actually a career exploration activity from the first semester of high school.

其实是高一上的职业体验活动。

I greatly enjoy such activities, where I can explore and become familiar with a profession frequently mentioned in daily life, alongside familiar friends. While many people think of “firefighter” when considering careers, few truly understand the realities and routines of this role. On this journey, we not only listened to firefighters share their personal experiences and moments on the brink of a fire but also had the chance to directly experience the urgency of firefighting ourselves.

我很喜欢这样的活动，和熟悉的朋友一起，接触并能够熟悉一个在日常中总被提起的职业。虽说很多人在考虑职业时，脑海中会浮现“消防员”三字，可是事实上，大部分人并不真正了解作为消防员所经历的工作与日程。在这趟旅途下，我们不仅听了消防员们讲述自己的亲身经历，那些濒临火场的瞬间，我们还切身体验救火的瞬间。

Visiting the fire museum, we were immersed in captivating and heroic tales of firefighting, along with detailed exhibits of the equipment used by firefighters.

参观消防博物馆，其中讲述了许多动人而英勇的救火事迹，以及消防员所用的器材，详细而沉浸。

(Leftmost: Me, photographing…)

（最左：我，在拍照）

The most impressive part of the entire experience followed. We entered the information-sharing command platform of the fire station. Utilizing big data and real-time satellite monitoring technology, the platform oversees the entire region, continuously tracking potential fire hazards and deploying firefighters directly online. The headquarters and the teams maintain seamless communication, and with the help of remote sensing technology, flawless deployment is achieved. This demonstration and operation left a profound impression on me. It showcased how big data technology can be directly connected to and impact human life.

之后是我整场体验中，最为印象深刻的一个部分。我们进入了本消防站的信息分享指挥平台。运用大数据和卫星实时监测播报技术，形成全区的管控，实时监测可能出现的火情，并且线上直接调动消防员出动，总部与分队可以做到保持联络，通过总部的遥感技术，更实现完美而不出纰漏的部署。这样的演示与操作，令我印象十分深刻。在运用大数据技术时，真正能够做到与人类的生活相关。

Experiencing a fire hose was truly a once-in-a-lifetime experience~

体验消防水枪，绝对是不可多得的经历啦~

Such activities are undeniably immensely rewarding. Gaining an in-depth understanding of a firefighter’s daily work reveals that their efforts are even more demanding and heroic than we often realize. No matter what profession we pursue in the future, it’s crucial to remember that every job deserves respect.

这样的活动，绝对是令人受益匪浅的。深入地了解到消防员的日常工作才发现，他们比我们所熟知的，还要更加辛苦与伟大。无论我们未来从事何种职业，都要知道，无论何种职业，都应当值得尊重。

Drama Elective Course戏剧选修课

In the first semester of my freshman year, I enrolled in a drama elective course. It turned out to be an incredibly memorable experience.

高一上我报名了戏剧选修课。这是一个令人非常难忘的课程。

Unlike other courses where we sit at desks, take notes, and study diligently, our classroom didn’t even require desks and chairs. On the first day, our wonderfully charming European teacher, Irene, instructed us to sit directly on the floor. We formed a circle, instantly bringing us much closer together.

不同于其他坐在课桌前翻开笔记对着课件勤学苦读的课程，我们的教室甚至不需要桌椅。第一节课，我们特别可爱的来自欧洲的老师Irene就告诉我们，直接席地而坐。我们围成一个圈，彼此的距离一下就拉近了许多。

In the initial classes, we didn’t study with specific goals in mind; instead, we embraced our natural spontaneity. It seems fitting to say that we escaped the burdens of heavy coursework and let our youthful energy shine in drama class. We played games with Irene, experiencing the world anew through a child’s perspective. At the same time, we learned through play, engaging in numerous exercises with lines and dialogue.

在前几节课，我们并没有有目的地在学习，而是在，释放天性。我想这么说很合适，从繁重的课业中逃脱抽身出来的我们，在戏剧课上释放作为孩子的活泼气息。我们围着Irene玩游戏，尽情地以孩子的视角再去体验这个世界。同时，我们也以玩代学，在这之中做了许多台词练习。

Later on, we began to appreciate renowned plays. The main event then commenced, as we learned how to write scripts, laying the strongest foundation for timeless theater. Not only did we write, but we also created storyboards, which I absolutely loved and found endlessly satisfying.

再到后来，我们开始欣赏一些著名的戏剧。重头戏由此开始，我们学习如何编写剧本，为一场经久不息的戏剧打下最坚实的基础。我们不仅写，也会画分镜镜头，而这是我所爱的，我对此乐此不疲。

The final challenge of the course was to create a short film of about five minutes. For us, already familiar with scriptwriting, this was not a difficult task. As we embraced our creativity, we were inspired with fantastical story ideas, leading to a swift creation of the script. The filming process was even more enjoyable.

课程的最终考验，是制作5分钟左右的微电影。这对于已经熟悉如何编写剧本的我们并不是难事。在释放天性的过程中，我们也更加拥有奇幻故事的灵感，于是剧本很快地被创作出来了，拍摄的过程更是有趣。

选修课-建筑与艺术

‘Architecture & Art’: Practice and cognition’ —optional course from Nanjing Foreign Language School the second semester of senior one

“建筑艺术：实践与认知”——NFLS高一年级第二学期选修课

在学习这门课的过程中，如同其标题，我投入了大量实践并结合了自己的认知，用我所拥有的对于艺术的理解拓宽着建筑与艺术融合之美。

Art and design firmly grasp the roots of architecture. Design nourishes architecture, enabling it to thrive and grow. All of this is inseparable from art; one could say that human life itself is the enduring gem of art.

    建筑像一棵苍天大树，它的枝干绵延至全方面多领域，向各个有前景的方向抛出橄榄枝。而牢牢抓紧建筑根基的，则是艺术与设计。设计为建筑提供养分，使其茁壮成长。这一切都与艺术密不可分，可以说，人类的生命正是艺术不朽的瑰宝。

    Practice Above All 实践至上

    Having theoretical knowledge and insights alone is not sufficient. Here, we need practical hands-on experience. Discussing architecture without models is not very advisable. So, let’s create a model.

    光有理论性知识和见解，自然是不够的。此处，我们需要动手实操。脱离模型谈建筑，不大可取。那么就做一个模型吧。

    This is one of the simplest yet rich in architectural knowledge models. Following material requirements, the walls are made of eco-friendly foam boards. When designing the integration of walls and ceilings, we delved into various meticulous approaches. For instance, we explored integrated streamlined designs, nested patterns, straight cuts, and so forth. Each method varies in aesthetic appeal and suitability for different applications. Without getting hands-on and doing it ourselves, many potential creative ideas might remain undiscovered or overlooked.

    这便是最为简易而富有建筑艺术知识的模型其一。根据材料学需要，墙体采用环保泡沫纸板。在设计墙壁与天花板的镶嵌形态时，我们进行了颇为考究的课题。例如，顶壁流线结合式，嵌套式，直切式，等等等等。不同的方式，美观程度不尽相同，运用场景也有异同之处。如果不是上手亲自去做，或许许多可能的创意就会被埋藏。

    (Left: Model, Right: 3D Stereoscopic Painting)（左：模型 右：3D立体画）

    Enjoy the pleasure and pride of carefully placing one’s own work.

    享受将自己的作品认真放置好的愉悦与自豪。

    Architecture is the learned game correct and magnificent of forms assembled in the light.

    建筑是光线下形状正确、绝妙又神奇的游戏。

    ​ ——Le Corbusier 勒·柯布西耶

    The effects brought by environment and light complement the art of architecture. The pursuit of enduring themes in architectural art includes thriving harmoniously with nature and coexisting in harmony.

    环境和光影所带来的效果与建筑的艺术相得益彰。与自然欣欣向荣，和谐共生的，也是建筑艺术所追求的经久不衰的论题。

    “A Light exists in Spring
    Not present on the Year
    At any other period –
    When March is scarcely here
    A Color stands abroad”

    “春日里的光
    一年的其他时间
    从不存在

    Final Thesis Presentation最终答辩

    I can vividly remember those few minutes standing in front of the blackboard, representing my work. As I articulated my understanding of architecture and art, which may have been naive, immature, or still infused with innocence, I felt like I was presenting my own art. I knew those thoughts came from my mind, a part of my soul. Perhaps looking back on this moment in the future will still feel inexperienced, but my understanding of architectural art is also a dynamic process of growth, much like life itself.

    我可以清晰地记得，当我站在黑板前，代表我的作品的那几分钟。当我清晰流利地阐述我或许稚嫩或许不成熟或许还尚存天真的对于建筑与艺术的理解时，我代表我自己的艺术。我知道他们来自我的脑海，是我灵魂的一部分。也许未来回望这一刻还是青涩，但我对于建筑艺术的理解，也是新生成长的动态过程，一如其本身。

    老师对我说：

    My tutor said to me:

    “From your design, I see a 16-year-old girl’s life filled with vibrant colors. It exudes youthfulness and bursts with creativity. What captivates in architectural design often lies in the stories behind it and the soul that governs it, and your soul is perhaps quite intriguing.”

    “我从你的设计中，读到的是一个生活充满鲜艳色彩的16岁女孩。青春洋溢，创造力勃发。建筑设计吸引人之处，多存在其背后的故事和主宰的灵魂，你的灵魂也许很有趣。”

    At sixteen, I am fortunate to have become acquainted with architecture and art. It is an honor to have developed a sincere understanding of them in my heart.

    16岁的我，十分有幸与建筑，与艺术相识。荣幸至此，也刻下心底对其诚挚的理解。
