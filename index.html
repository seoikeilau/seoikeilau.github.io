My Hong Kong University Summer Institute关于我的HKUSI夏校

My first experience attending a summer school was both special and precious to me. I have always been drawn to Hong Kong—perhaps it’s the unique charm I sensed in the TV dramas and movies I watched as a child that captivated me. The city has always held a great allure for me. I love wandering through its streets, immersed in the dazzling neon lights, and I equally cherish the serene and profound atmosphere hidden beneath the city’s bustling surface.

My memories of the University of Hong Kong (HKU) date back to a visit during my fifth year of primary school. At that time, my impression was still quite shallow, but I was struck by how this university could blend the old with the new, embodying both solemn elegance and vibrant freedom. Since then, I have harbored a deep affection for HKU. Returning to this campus now as a summer student, I still feel a sense of honor and happiness.

第一次参加夏校的经历，对我而言很特殊也很珍贵。我喜欢香港这座城市，也许是小时候看过的港剧电影里那种别样的风情吸引着我，香港于我而言一直有极大的吸引力。我喜欢漫步在其街头霓虹灯下的绚烂沉浮，也同样喜欢它藏在市井之下的静谧厚重。对于港大的记忆，停留在小学五年级去参观的经历，那时候尚为浅薄的印象里，只觉得原来有这么一所学校，可以结合古老与新颖，同时集庄重典雅与自由明媚于一身。自那时起，便对HKU埋下了深切好感的种子。再一番踏入这所校园，以暑期学生的身份，我仍觉得荣幸与幸福。

From July 23 to August 2, 2024, during the two weeks of study, I stayed at HKU’s student dormitory: Jockey Club Student Village III. I fully immersed myself in the daily life of an HKU student, savoring the various foods on campus and spending time browsing through the souvenir shops. Most importantly, I learned the unique study methods and ways of thinking that are characteristic of HKU students. Although it was only two weeks, the experience was incredibly enriching.

2024.7.23~2024.8.2，两周的学习时间，我住在港大的学生宿舍：赛马会第三学生村，沉浸式体验作为一名港大学生的日常生活作息，吃遍港大校园美食，在纪念品商店流连忘返，最重要地，也学习到了独一无二的，属于港大学生的那种学习方式与思考模式。短短两周，受益匪浅。

About the course关于课程

I participated in one of the streams of the Future Innovator in STEM series: AI and the Virtual World. STEM itself integrates science, technology, engineering, and mathematics, with a strong emphasis on professionalism and academic rigor. The AI stream, in particular, is one of the areas I’m most interested in. In my view, with the current pace of technological advancement, artificial intelligence is bound to play a crucial role in the future, becoming an indispensable part of human life. Such a forward-looking field is, in my opinion, well worth exploring. Amidst the rapid technological progress of humanity, the exploration of sustainable development for the future is the core issue studied through STEM.

我参加的是Future Innovator in STEM系列课程中的其中一个stream: AI and the Virtual World。STEM本身是一个结合科学科技工程数学于一体的内容，有着很强的专业性和学术性，而关于AI的此stream更是我所感兴趣的领域之一。在我看来，以现在世界的科技发展水平，人工智能必定在未来占据很重要的地位，作为人类的生活无可缺少的一大领域，如此有前瞻性的领域，我认为是很值得深究的。在人类日新月异飞速发展的科技之中，探索人类未来可持续性发展之道，是通过STEM所研究的核心问题。

“The programme Future Innovator in STEM focuses on cross-discipline learning, that combines Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics. The programme begins with a foundation course on the concept of the design process, Artificial Intelligence, and Big data analysis which can develop the basic skillsets for the cycle of “from ideas to products”. Besides, students could consolidate their knowledge learned from different subjects to develop problem-solving and critical-thinking skills.”

“Future Innovator in STEM”专注于跨学科学习，结合了科学、技术、工程和数学。该计划从设计过程概念、人工智能和大数据分析的基础课程开始，旨在培养“从创意到产品”循环所需的基本技能。此外，学生还可以通过整合从不同学科中学到的知识，发展解决问题和批判性思维的能力。

Understanding Virtual Worlds and AI Integration in STEM

  1. Introduction to Virtual Worlds:
    • Virtual worlds are not just digital environments but a blend of both imaginary and real elements. They range from mirror worlds (digital twins of our physical reality) to alternate, fantasy, and alien worlds.
    • These worlds can exist in various forms such as games, cinema, literature, and more, each offering different levels of immersion and interactivity.
  2. Role of AI in Virtual Worlds:
    • AI plays a crucial role in the creation and operation of virtual worlds. It can generate 3D models, simulate environments, and enhance interactivity.
    • AI is not just a tool for creating virtual worlds but also an integrated part of these worlds, influencing how they evolve and interact with users.
  3. The Human Imagination and Virtual Reality:
    • The development of virtual worlds is deeply connected to human imagination. Historical examples like Chinese landscape paintings show that the concept of virtual reality has long existed in art and culture.
    • Modern virtual worlds extend this tradition, offering new ways to explore and express human creativity.
  4. Technological Tools and Workflow:
    • Various tools like Unreal Engine, TwinMotion, and AI-based systems are used to create and manipulate virtual worlds. These tools allow for the creation of highly detailed and interactive environments.
    • The workflow involves a combination of coding, 3D modeling, animation, and AI integration, requiring interdisciplinary knowledge and skills.

Reflections and Thoughts: the benefits and practical applications of virtual worlds for humanity?

  • Imagination as a Driver: The concept of virtual worlds emphasizes the power of human imagination. In a sense, the tools and technologies we develop are extensions of our creative potential. This raises questions about the future possibilities of virtual worlds—could they one day become indistinguishable from reality?
  • AI and Ethics: The integration of AI in virtual worlds opens up ethical considerations. As AI becomes more sophisticated, the line between the virtual and real may blur, posing challenges in areas like privacy, identity, and the nature of human interaction.
  • Educational Applications: Virtual worlds and AI have significant potential in education. They can create immersive learning experiences that go beyond traditional methods, offering students a deeper understanding of complex concepts.

理解虚拟世界与AI在STEM中的结合

关于虚拟世界：

    • 虚拟世界不仅是数字环境，更是虚拟与现实元素的融合。它们涵盖了镜像世界（物理现实的数字孪生）、替代世界、幻想世界和外星世界等不同类型。
    • 这些世界可以存在于游戏、电影、文学等多种形式中，每种形式提供不同层次的沉浸感和互动性。
  2. AI在虚拟世界中的作用：
    • AI在虚拟世界的创建和运行中起着关键作用。它可以生成3D模型、模拟环境并增强互动性。
    • AI不仅是创造虚拟世界的工具，也是这些世界的组成部分，影响着它们的发展和与用户的互动。
  3. 人类想象力与虚拟现实：
    • 虚拟世界的发展与人类的想象力密切相关。历史上的例子如中国山水画显示，虚拟现实的概念早已存在于艺术和文化中。
    • 现代虚拟世界延续了这一传统，提供了探索和表达人类创造力的新方式。
  4. 技术工具与工作流程：
    • 使用Unreal Engine、TwinMotion等各种工具以及基于AI的系统来创建和操控虚拟世界。这些工具允许创建高度详细和互动的环境。
    • 工作流程结合了编码、3D建模、动画和AI整合，要求跨学科的知识和技能。

收获与思考：虚拟世界为人类带来的收获与实例应用？

  • 想象力的驱动力: 虚拟世界的概念强调了人类想象力的力量。从某种意义上说，我们开发的工具和技术是我们创造潜力的延伸。这引发了关于虚拟世界未来可能性的思考——它们是否有一天会与现实难以区分？
  • AI与伦理: AI在虚拟世界中的整合引发了伦理考量。随着AI变得更加复杂，虚拟与现实之间的界限可能会模糊，从而在隐私、身份和人类互动性质等方面带来挑战。
  • 教育应用: 虚拟世界和AI在教育中具有巨大潜力。它们可以创造超越传统方法的沉浸式学习体验，为学生提供对复杂概念更深入的理解。

Final Project 结课项目

Unlike what we had anticipated, we didn’t have a traditional presentation. Perhaps this is because theoretical knowledge in STEM is merely the surface, and deeper research requires practical application. Our project involved creating a 4.5-minute video, entirely conceived and produced by AI. It might seem like a simple task, where you just provide content to the AI and let it do the rest, but that’s far from the truth. As the ‘director team,’ the scenes and structure we envisioned in our minds required training the appropriate AI models to match the scenes, lighting, and characters. This is not a simple task. Achieving high quality, visual refinement, and narrative completeness requires continuous experimentation and adjustments, as well as planning and perseverance.

不同于预先所想的，我们并没有迎来一个presentation，或许是因为理论的东西在STEM中仅仅是表层，更深入的研究需要实践代替，于是我们的项目是完成一个4分半的视频，独立构思，全程由AI进行创作构成。看似是简单的工作，或许只需要将内容告诉AI任其自行发挥即可？那当然错了。作为“导演组”，我们所想要的画面与结构，都在脑海中有所浮现，要训练适合的AI模型去完成场景，光影，人像的契合，并不是一件简单的事。追求完成度，画面的精巧度与故事的完整性，需要不断地去尝试与调整，也需要计划和韧性。

    当今社会科技的快速发展惠及着多重领域，建筑正是其一。科技的发展源于人类驾驭工具能力的进步，为人类生活提供着更高层次的体验感与便捷，拓宽了人们的想象界限。我们不免听说诸如“AI科技房”等词汇，这是人工智能将普及到千家万户的预示和写照。

    Architecture, like a mighty tree reaching out its branches in all directions, extends into various fields, offering olive branches to promising directions. Art and design firmly grasp the roots of architecture. Design nourishes architecture, enabling it to thrive and grow. All of this is inseparable from art; one could say that human life itself is the enduring gem of art.

    建筑像一棵苍天大树，它的枝干绵延至全方面多领域，向各个有前景的方向抛出橄榄枝。而牢牢抓紧建筑根基的，则是艺术与设计。设计为建筑提供养分，使其茁壮成长。这一切都与艺术密不可分，可以说，人类的生命正是艺术不朽的瑰宝。

    Practice Above All 实践至上

    Having theoretical knowledge and insights alone is not sufficient. Here, we need practical hands-on experience. Discussing architecture without models is not very advisable. So, let’s create a model.

    光有理论性知识和见解，自然是不够的。此处，我们需要动手实操。脱离模型谈建筑，不大可取。那么就做一个模型吧。

    This is one of the simplest yet rich in architectural knowledge models. Following material requirements, the walls are made of eco-friendly foam boards. When designing the integration of walls and ceilings, we delved into various meticulous approaches. For instance, we explored integrated streamlined designs, nested patterns, straight cuts, and so forth. Each method varies in aesthetic appeal and suitability for different applications. Without getting hands-on and doing it ourselves, many potential creative ideas might remain undiscovered or overlooked.

    这便是最为简易而富有建筑艺术知识的模型其一。根据材料学需要，墙体采用环保泡沫纸板。在设计墙壁与天花板的镶嵌形态时，我们进行了颇为考究的课题。例如，顶壁流线结合式，嵌套式，直切式，等等等等。不同的方式，美观程度不尽相同，运用场景也有异同之处。如果不是上手亲自去做，或许许多可能的创意就会被埋藏。

    I can vividly remember those few minutes standing in front of the blackboard, representing my work.     我可以清晰地记得，当我站在黑板前，代表我的作品的那几分钟。当我清晰流利地阐述我或许稚嫩或许不成熟或许还尚存天真的对于建筑与艺术的理解时，我代表我自己的艺术。我知道他们来自我的脑海，是我灵魂的一部分。也许未来回望这一刻还是青涩，但我对于建筑艺术的理解，也是新生成长的动态过程，一如其本身。
